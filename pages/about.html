About me:


My name is Max Osborne. Max is short for Maxine, which comes from my grandmother. I model because I love the way that art can make you feel, whether that be creating it or observing it. I believe that when collaboration occurs between artists, their work can reach higher peaks, and touch more people. I have done glamour, fashion, fashion shows, art nude, brand representation, extra work on a film, stage-acting (when younger), and I also write occasionally. I tend to be easy-going, low maintenance, and creative. I have a passion for all that I do, and I work hard toward the goals that I set for myself. I am always open for any input on a concept when I am working with anyone.

If you have any questions about who I am, what I do, or for booking, please contact me on any of my forums.
